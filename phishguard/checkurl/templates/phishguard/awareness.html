General Phishing Awareness Tips

  • Look out for unfamiliar sender email addresses.
  • Do not click on suspicious links or attachments.
  • Always check the URL of a website for spelling errors or unusual characters.
  • Make sure a website uses HTTPS and shows a secure connection.
  • If you are unsure, contact the company directly via their official website.
  • Use strong passwords and two-factor authentication.
  • Stay cautious on public Wi-Fi.

Types of Phishing Attacks

  • Spear Phishing
  • Clone Phishing
  • Whaling
  • Smishing

Tips for Employers

  • Alert employees to the risks.
  • Use security software and tools.
  • Develop a culture of awareness.
  • Identify available training resources and train employees how to spot phishing.
